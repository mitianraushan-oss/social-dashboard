Upload Your TCX Files to Strava

Strava
1 Go to strava.com and log in (or open the Strava mobile app)
2 Click the orange + button and select Upload Activity
3 Select the .tcx files from the ZIP you downloaded — you can upload up to 25 at a time
4 Strava will process each file and add it to your activity feed
lightbulb On web: drag and drop files directly. On mobile: extract the ZIP first, then select individual .tcx files.
